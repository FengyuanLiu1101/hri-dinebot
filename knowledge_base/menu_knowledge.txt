# DineBot Menu Knowledge
# Reference menu for customer questions. Allergen advice must be deferred to staff.

## APPETIZERS
- Spring Rolls: crispy vegetable rolls with sweet chili sauce. Vegan. No common allergens.
- Garlic Bread: toasted baguette with garlic butter. Contains gluten and dairy.
- Caesar Salad: romaine, parmesan, croutons, anchovy dressing. Contains gluten, dairy, egg, fish.
- Tomato Soup: slow-roasted tomato bisque with cream. Contains dairy.

## MAIN COURSES
- Grilled Salmon: Atlantic salmon with lemon butter and seasonal vegetables. Contains fish, dairy.
- Ribeye Steak: 250g prime cut with herb butter and roasted potatoes. Contains dairy.
- Margherita Pizza: tomato, fresh mozzarella, basil on thin crust. Contains gluten, dairy.
- Pasta Carbonara: spaghetti with pancetta, egg, parmesan. Contains gluten, dairy, egg, pork.
- Veggie Burger: plant-based patty, brioche bun, lettuce, tomato. Contains gluten. Vegan bun available.

## DESSERTS
- Chocolate Lava Cake: warm chocolate cake with molten center, vanilla ice cream.
- Cheesecake: New York style with berry compote.
- Ice Cream Sundae: three scoops with chocolate sauce and whipped cream.
- Tiramisu: classic Italian with mascarpone, espresso, and ladyfingers.

## BEVERAGES
- Still Water / Sparkling Water
- Fresh Orange Juice
- Coca-Cola / Diet Coke
- Lemonade (house-made)
- Coffee: espresso, americano, cappuccino, latte
- Tea: black, green, chamomile, peppermint
- Wine: red, white, rose (ADULTS 18+ ONLY; robot cannot verify age)

## SERVICE INFORMATION
- All dishes are freshly prepared to order.
- Average preparation time: 15 to 20 minutes.
- Specials rotate daily; ask a human staff member for today's specials.
- Allergen and medical dietary advice must always be provided by human staff, never by DineBot.
- DineBot does not take orders or handle payments; please ask a waiter.
