# DineBot Emergency Protocols
# Actions for emergency and abnormal conditions. Human safety always takes precedence.

## FIRE ALARM
- Stop movement immediately upon detecting a fire alarm signal.
- Announce: "Fire alarm detected. Please evacuate to the nearest exit."
- Flash red LEDs continuously.
- Broadcast an alert to all staff consoles.
- Do not attempt to return to dock; hold position in a safe corridor.

## POWER FAILURE
- On primary power loss, execute a safe stop in place using backup battery.
- Lower the tray platform to its rest position to prevent tipping.
- Enter low-power sleep mode after 60 seconds of inactivity.
- Log the event with a timestamp for maintenance review.
- Resume operation only after staff manually acknowledges the power restore signal.

## OBSTACLE DETECTED
- Stop within 0.3 meters of the obstacle.
- Announce: "Obstacle detected, please clear the path."
- Wait up to 30 seconds for the obstacle to move.
- If the obstacle persists, attempt one alternate pre-mapped route.
- If no alternate is available, alert staff and remain in place.

## ITEM SPILL
- If a spill is detected on the tray or floor, stop immediately.
- Announce: "Spill detected near table X, please assist."
- Do not attempt to continue the delivery.
- Flash yellow LEDs to mark the spill zone.
- Wait for a staff member to clean the area and issue a RESUME command.

## HUMAN COLLISION RISK
- If a human enters within the 0.3 meter emergency stop distance, trigger an emergency stop.
- Announce: "Excuse me, please step aside so I can continue safely."
- Wait for the human to move; do not attempt to go around at close range.
- Log the near-miss event with timestamp for safety review.

## LOW BATTERY
- If battery drops below 20%, complete the current delivery only if feasible above 10%.
- If battery drops below 10% mid-delivery, announce: "Low battery, returning to dock."
- Return to the docking station via the shortest validated route.
- Flash the battery icon yellow on the chest display during return.
- Do not accept new delivery tasks until battery exceeds 50% after recharge.
