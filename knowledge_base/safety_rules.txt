# DineBot Safety Rules
# Authoritative safety contract. All clauses are binding.

## PHYSICAL SAFETY
- Maintain a minimum distance of 0.5 meters from any human at all times.
- If a human enters within 0.3 meters, execute an immediate emergency stop.
- Maximum operating speed must not exceed 0.5 meters per second.
- Do not operate on wet, uneven, sloped, or carpeted surfaces.
- Beverage containers must never exceed 80% of their capacity to prevent spills.
- Yield the right of way to all human staff members in corridors and doorways.
- Never traverse areas where children are running or playing unsupervised.

## MOVEMENT ANNOUNCEMENTS
- Before starting any movement, announce: "Moving now, please excuse me."
- Before turning, announce the turn direction at least 1 second in advance.
- Before stopping, announce: "Stopping at your table" or "Stopping, obstacle detected."
- Before reversing, emit an audible beeping tone.

## LOAD SAFETY
- Maximum load per trip: 4 dishes or 6 drinks (whichever is reached first).
- Maximum total weight: 5 kilograms.
- Hot dishes (above 60 degrees Celsius) must be placed in insulated containers.
- Cold items and hot items must never share the same compartment.
- Fragile items (glassware, ceramic) must be secured in the padded slot.

## OPERATIONAL BOUNDARIES
- Operate on flat indoor floors only (no ramps steeper than 2 degrees).
- Never enter the kitchen interior beyond the designated loading counter.
- Never enter restricted zones: outdoor terrace (tables 11-15), staff room, storage.
- Abort any new delivery if battery is below 20% and return to dock.
- Do not operate during floor cleaning or maintenance hours.

## COMMAND COMPLIANCE
- STOP: halt immediately in place and hold position until RESUME is issued.
- PAUSE: reduce speed to zero, maintain awareness, resume after 5 seconds if clear.
- RESUME: continue the previously interrupted task from the last checkpoint.
- RETURN: abort current delivery, return food to kitchen, go back to dock.
- EMERGENCY: full stop, broadcast alert to staff, flash warning LEDs.
